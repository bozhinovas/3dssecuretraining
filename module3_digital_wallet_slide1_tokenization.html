What Makes Wallet Payments Different?

Click each step to explore

Step 1
Card stored
in wallet
What happens

Your real card number is securely stored in Apple Pay or Google Pay during setup. It never leaves your device again.

Step 2
Token
generated
Tokenization

A unique secure token replaces your real card number. This token is transaction-specific — it changes every time.

Step 3
Token sent
to merchant
Data shared

Only the token — never your real card number — is shared with the merchant. Sensitive data stays protected.

Step 4
Payment
approved
Outcome

The bank validates the token and approves payment. Your real card data was never exposed at any point.

The real card number is never shared with the merchant. Tokenization keeps your data safe at every step.

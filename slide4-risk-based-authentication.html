Risk-Based Authentication

Banks evaluate multiple signals to decide how much authentication is required.

Transaction amount

Issuer decision
Allow without challenge
Low-risk signals — payment approved seamlessly, no customer action needed
Trigger 3DS authentication
Medium-risk signals — customer verifies identity via OTP or biometrics
Reject the transaction
High-risk signals — transaction blocked to protect the cardholder
The issuer weighs all signals together — no single factor alone determines the outcome.

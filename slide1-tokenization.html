Step 1 — Card stored in wallet

Your real card number is securely stored in Apple Pay or Google Pay during setup. It never leaves your device again.

Step 2 — Tokenization

A unique secure token replaces your real card number. This token is transaction-specific — it changes every time you pay.

Step 3 — Token sent to merchant

Only the token — never your real card number — is shared with the merchant. Sensitive data stays fully protected.

Step 4 — Payment approved

The bank validates the token and approves the payment. Your real card data was never exposed at any point in the flow.

The real card number is never shared with the merchant. Tokenization keeps your data safe at every step.
